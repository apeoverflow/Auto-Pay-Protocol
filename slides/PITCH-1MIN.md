>
> AutoPay Protocol fixes this. We're non-custodial recurring payments on USDC. Think Stripe subscriptions, but on-chain — no intermediary ever touches your money. Merchants keep 97.5 cents on every dollar instead of 65. Settlement is instant. And no one can shut you down, because your subscription logic lives on a smart contract.
>

>
> But here's what makes us different from every other payments protocol: we're also the subscription layer for AI agents. Agents with wallets can discover services through HTTP 402, subscribe, and pay autonomously — no human in the loop. Two lines of code. As agentic commerce explodes, every agent will need a way to pay for APIs, data feeds, and compute. We're building that rail.
>
> Come to our booth — I'll show you a live agent subscribing and paying in under thirty seconds.

## Audience-Specific Adjustments

### For Investors
- Emphasize: 2.5% take rate, 50% cheaper than Stripe, market size of subscription economy ($275B+)
- Say: "We capture 2.5% of every recurring charge on-chain. The subscription economy is $275 billion. We're live today."
- They care about: business model, TAM, defensibility, traction

### For Builders / Hackers
- Emphasize: two lines of code, the SDK, MCP server, HTTP 402 discovery protocol
- Say: "Wrap fetch once. 402 discovery, subscription, and retry are all transparent. Your agent just calls fetch."
- They care about: developer experience, integration simplicity, open source

### For Crypto Enthusiasts
- Emphasize: non-custodial, censorship-resistant, no KYC, your keys your revenue
- Say: "No one holds your money. No one can freeze your account. Your subscription logic is a smart contract — it runs forever."
- They care about: decentralization, sovereignty, anti-censorship

## Delivery Tips

1. **Open with the story, not your name.** People stop for stories, not pitches.
2. **Make eye contact.** You're at a booth, not on a stage.
3. **Read the badge.** Investor? Builder? Adjust your emphasis.
4. **End with a question.** "What are you building?" or "Have you dealt with payment deplatforming?" — turns pitch into conversation.
5. **Have the demo queued.** Show a live agent subscribing on Base in real time. Nothing sells like seeing it work.
6. **Don't explain blockchain.** This is ETH Global. They know.
7. **Speak in concrete numbers.** "97.5 cents on the dollar" hits harder than "lower fees."
8. **Practice until it feels like a conversation**, not a recitation.
