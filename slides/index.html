Smart Contract

Policy-Based Payment Enforcement

PolicyManager.sol

createPolicy()

Subscriber sets amount, interval, cap. First charge executes immediately.

charge()

Relayer calls when due. USDC split: 97.5% merchant, 2.5% protocol.

revokePolicy()

Instant cancellation. Revoked on-chain, no merchant approval needed.

cancelFailedPolicy()

After 3 consecutive failures, anyone can terminate the policy.
